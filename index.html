Meet Wicked—the very good girl who was just named America's best working dog
A three-year-old Doberman Pinscher, Wicked, clinched the win for the American Kennel Club's 2023 Working Group competition Sunday evening. Exactly the opposite of what her name might suggest, Wicked has a calm but commanding presence.
